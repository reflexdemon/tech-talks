## What is Spec Driven Development?
- Spec-driven development (SDD) is a methodology where machine-readable specifications (e.g., Markdown, OpenAPI) act as the primary, executable source of truth, from which code, tests, and documentation are generated.

## What is it for AI First development

- Replaces ad-hoc `vibe coding` with a structured approach, allowing AI agents to follow a, verified plan
- Provides a more predictable and reliable way to build software

# Spec: Intro to SDD

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Contrast Vibe Coding vs. Spec-Driven Development
The presentation must clearly define "Vibe Coding" (ad-hoc AI prompting) and its pitfalls, contrasted with the reliability of SDD.

#### Scenario: Explaining Vibe Coding pitfalls
- **WHEN** Presenting the "Vibe Coding" slide
- **THEN** The slide must list at least 3 pitfalls: inconsistency, lack of long-term maintainability, and "hidden" logic.

#### Scenario: Explaining SDD benefits
- **WHEN** Presenting the "Why SDD?" slide
- **THEN** The slide must highlight machine-readable specs as the source of truth and predictable AI behavior.
